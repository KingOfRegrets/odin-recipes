Lasagna Recipe

Layer upon layer of rich flavor defines this timeless lasagna recipe. At its core, a slow-simmered meat sauce made with ground beef, onions, garlic, tomatoes, and a touch of red wine sets the foundation. This is layered between tender sheets of pasta, a creamy b√©chamel or ricotta mixture enriched with egg and Parmesan, and generous amounts of mozzarella cheese.

Baked until golden and bubbling, this lasagna delivers a perfect balance of savory meat, velvety cheese, and al dente pasta in every bite. Serve with a side of garlic bread and a green salad for a comforting, crowd-pleasing meal that's just as satisfying the next day.

Ingredients

  • Meat Sauce
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 2 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 lb ground beef
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1/2 tsp pepper
    • 24 oz pasta sauce
    • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Cheese Filling
    • 15 oz ricotta cheese
    • 1 egg
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Other
    • 9 lasagna noodles
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
    • Heat oil in a pan
    • Cook onion and garlic for 2-3 minutes
    • Add ground beef, salt, and pepper. Cook until brown
    • add pasta sauce and seasoning. Simmer for 10-15 minutes
  2. Mix cheese filling
      3. In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, and Parmesan
  3. Cook noodles
    • Boil according to package directions. Drain
  4. Assemble in baking dish
    • Spread a little sauce on the bottom
    • Add 3 noddles -> 1/3 of ricotta mix -> sauce -> mozzarella
    • Repeat 3 layers. Finish with sauce and mozarrella on top
  5. Bake
    • Cover with foil and bake at 375 Degrees Farenheit for 25 minutes
    • Uncover and bake 15-20 more minutes
    • Let rest 10 minutes before serving
